Who are you?

Do you have a job? Do you want one? CO-OP is for you. This site is for all working-class Americans. We make up the majority of Americans and it is time for us to stop letting other people boss us around!

Who are we?

At CO-OP we hope you will learn more about an alternative to capitalistic coorporations, with one powerful person sitting at the top making all the decisions about how much of the company profits they are willing to share with you for your hard work.

What is our goal?

Our primary goal is education. We want to inform users of what a worker co-op is. We want to show examples of successful co-ops and encourage a greater number of these worker-driven entities to exist. We want to let Americans know that you can absolutely be trusted with your own destiny. We want to help people come to the realization that you too can work in a democratic work environment. As Americans, we often call ourselves the "home of the free". We preach of the benifit and the liberty of democrocy, but corporations are inherantly anti-democratic.



Why are you here?

We encourage users to explore our database of American worker co-ops. You can also create an account and share a co-op that you know of, or are associated with, that is not yet in our list.
